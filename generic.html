More about my projects

An average Nigerian trashes approximately 189 kilograms of food every year totaling 37.9 million (37,941,470) tonnes of food every 12 months. Annually, out of a hundred percent, only 60% of foods produced are utilized, with approximately 40% going to waste, putting the country’s economy at great risk. The urge to make an impact and enlighten farmers about effective post-harvest practices is what brought about the development of the farm-to-market app. This app helps the regulation of over-produced food to limit waste. It provides first-hand information on best practices for food preservation and post-harvest techniques
